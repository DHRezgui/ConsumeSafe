Why Boycott? (BDS Movement)

BDS (Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions) is a Palestinian-led movement for rights and freedom. It calls for boycotts of companies complicit in Israeli occupation of Palestinian territories.

Three Principles of BDS:

  • 1. Boycott Companies supporting Israeli military/settlements
  • 2. Divestment Remove investments from such companies
  • 3. Sanctions Pressure governments to impose sanctions
